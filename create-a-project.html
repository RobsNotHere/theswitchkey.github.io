Select a payment method and get redirected to Stripe for secure payment processing. This payment will be used to search and match vendors that are qualified to work in your project. The process will take maximum one week.

  • A $1450 deposit will be required to start searching and matching. This will be deducted from the total if matched.

  • An additional $3500 will be required to connect with a manufacturer.
